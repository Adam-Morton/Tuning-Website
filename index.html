About Me

Hi, I’m Adam Morton, a professional piano tuner and technician based in Durham, Ontario. I’ve spent years helping pianos of all shapes and sizes sound their absolute best, whether for home musicians, schools, or performance venues. I take pride in combining precision, care, and a deep love of music in every tuning, repair, and maintenance session. From regulating action to voicing hammers, I ensure your piano responds beautifully and consistently.

In addition to tuning, I teach piano to students of all ages, helping them build strong fundamentals, develop musicality, and enjoy playing. Whether it’s a beginner learning their first notes or a young performer preparing for a recital, I provide lessons that are engaging, structured, and tailored to each student.

My goal is simple: to help your piano—and your playing—reach its fullest potential. I treat each instrument and student with the attention they deserve, so you can enjoy music with confidence and joy.
