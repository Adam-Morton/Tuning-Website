About Me

Hi, I’m Adam Morton, a piano tuner and musician based in Durham, Ontario. I’m passionate about helping pianos sound their best and bringing out the joy in every instrument. Every piano I work on gets my full attention — I focus on making sure the tuning is precise, the keys feel responsive, and the instrument is enjoyable to play. I approach every piano with care, patience, and a genuine love of music, ensuring the piano can be played and cherished for years to come.

In addition to tuning, I teach piano to students of all ages. My lessons focus on building strong fundamentals, developing musicality, and fostering a love for playing. I aim to make learning engaging and rewarding, whether a student is just starting out, preparing for a recital, or exploring music for fun. Each lesson is tailored to the student’s needs and learning style, so they feel supported and encouraged every step of the way.

Music has always been an important part of my life, and I love sharing that enthusiasm with others. My goal is simple: to help you and your piano make music that feels enjoyable and inspiring. I bring dedication, care, and a patient approach to every piano I tune and every student I teach, so you can play with confidence, improve steadily, and truly enjoy the experience of music.

Whether you’re a student looking to grow as a pianist, a piano owner wanting a reliable tuning, or simply someone who appreciates good sound, I’m here to help. I’m excited to continue developing my skills and to share that knowledge with every piano and student I work with.
